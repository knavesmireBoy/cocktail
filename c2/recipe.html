Sidecar

Method

Shake well with cracked ice, then strain into a chilled cocktail glass that has had its outside rim rubbed with lemon juice and dipped in sugar.

Recipe

  • Sidecar
  • 3/4 oz Cointreau
  • 3/4 oz lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 oz cognac
  • Cocktail Glass
