Cosmopolitan

Method

  • Shake ingredients well with cracked ice, then strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  • Some replace the Rose's with 1/2 ounce fresh lime juice and 1/2 ounce simple syrup, but that's taking the drink way too seriously. (Since the cranberry juice comes from sweetened concentrate, why not the lime?)

Recipe

  • Cosmopolitan
  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1 ounce Cointreau
  • 1 ounce cranberry juice
  • 1 ounce Rose's lime juice
